Aunt’s Kitchen – Requirements & Design Proposal

Project Overview

Purpose: To create a modern, easy-to-use website for my aunt’s homemade food business called Aunt’s Kitchen. The site will showcase her menu, food photos, specials, and allow customers to place inquiries or small local orders.

Intended Users:

  • Local customers looking for home-cooked meals
  • Event planners searching for catering options
  • Returning customers checking specials or contact info

Website Content:

  • Menu with categories and prices
  • Gallery of food photos
  • About section (story, values)
  • Order/contact form
  • Special offers section

Page Design Details

Home

  • Purpose: Welcome users, highlight featured dishes, show daily special.
  • Audience: All visitors.
  • Content: Hero banner, “Our Story” snippet, call-to-action buttons.
  • Actions: Buttons lead to Menu and Contact pages.

Menu

  • Purpose: Display dishes with categories and prices.
  • Audience: Customers ordering food.
  • Content: List or table of meals with images.
  • Interactivity: JavaScript filter by category (veg/non-veg, desserts, etc.).

Gallery

  • Purpose: Show photos of prepared food.
  • Interactivity: Lightbox image viewer (jQuery plugin).

About

  • Purpose: Introduce Aunt and the story behind the business.
  • Content: Biography, photos, mission statement.

Contact

  • Purpose: Let users send inquiries or small orders.
  • Content: Name, email, message form.
  • Interactivity: Form validation and thank-you pop-up.

Specials

  • Purpose: Show limited-time offers.
  • Content: Rotating offers updated weekly.
  • Interactivity: JavaScript slideshow banner.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Slideshow Banner: JS rotates special dish images on Home page.
  • Filterable Menu: Dropdown filters by dish type.
  • Gallery Lightbox: Enlarge photos when clicked.
  • Form Validation: JS checks for required fields before submitting.
  • AJAX Specials Loader: Pulls daily specials from a local JSON file.

Example Sites

  • Panera Bread – menu & gallery inspiration
  • Domino’s – rotating banners and interactivity
